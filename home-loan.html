Home Loan Documents

Used to buy a new or resale home, construct a house, or renovate an existing property.

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, rent agreement, or passport
  • Income Proof (Salaried): Last 3-6 months salary slips, Form 16, and 6 months bank statements
  • Income Proof (Self-Employed): ITR with computation for last 2-3 years, balance sheet, and bank statements
  • Property Documents: Sale deed, agreement to sell, approved building plan, occupancy certificate, and property tax receipts
  • Other: Passport-size photographs, employment letter, and processing fee cheque
Note: Requirements may vary by lender, loan amount, and applicant profile.
