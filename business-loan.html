Business Loan Documents

Fund working capital, purchase equipment, expand operations, or manage seasonal cash flow for your business.

  • KYC of Proprietor/Directors: Aadhaar, PAN, and address proof
  • Business Registration: GST certificate, Udyam/MSME registration, shop act license, or incorporation certificate
  • Financial Statements: ITR, balance sheet, and P&L for last 2-3 years
  • Bank Statements: Business current account statements for last 12 months
  • Business Proof: Partnership deed, MOA/AOA, or proprietorship declaration
  • Other: Business plan or project report, collateral documents if secured loan, and board resolution
Note: Requirements may vary by lender, loan amount, and applicant profile.
