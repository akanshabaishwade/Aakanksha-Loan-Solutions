What you need to apply

Required Documents For Each Loan Type

Keep these documents ready to speed up your loan approval. Additional documents may be requested based on your profile and loan amount.

Home Loan

Used to buy a new or resale home, construct a house, or renovate an existing property.

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, rent agreement, or passport
  • Income Proof (Salaried): Last 3–6 months salary slips, Form 16, and 6 months bank statements
  • Income Proof (Self-Employed): ITR with computation for last 2–3 years, balance sheet, and bank statements
  • Property Documents: Sale deed, agreement to sell, approved building plan, occupancy certificate, and property tax receipts
  • Other: Passport-size photographs, employment letter, and processing fee cheque

Home Loan Top Up

Extra loan amount over your running home loan — ideal for home improvement, education, or personal use.

  • Identity & Address Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, and current address proof
  • Existing Loan Details: Home loan account statement and sanction letter from current lender
  • Income Proof: Latest salary slips or ITR with bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Property Documents: Copy of title deed, latest property valuation report
  • Other: Purpose-of-loan declaration, photographs, and NOC from existing lender (if applicable)

Loan Against Property (LAP)

Mortgage your residential or commercial property to access a high-value loan at lower interest rates.

  • Identity & Address Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or voter ID
  • Property Documents: Title deed, chain of ownership, approved plan, and encumbrance certificate (EC)
  • Property Tax & Utility Bills: Latest receipts as proof of ownership and occupancy
  • Income Proof: Salary slips / ITR for 2–3 years with 6–12 months bank statements
  • Business Proof (if applicable): GST registration, shop act license, or partnership deed
  • Other: Valuation report, photographs, and loan application form

Commercial Loan

Finance purchase or construction of commercial property, or fund business operations linked to commercial assets.

  • KYC Documents: Aadhaar, PAN, and address proof of all applicants and guarantors
  • Business Proof: Company registration, GST certificate, partnership deed, or MOA/AOA
  • Financial Documents: ITR, audited financials, and profit & loss statements for last 2–3 years
  • Bank Statements: Business and personal accounts for last 12 months
  • Property Documents: Sale deed, approved commercial plan, and NOC from relevant authorities
  • Other: Business profile, project report, and board resolution (for companies)

Personal Loan

Collateral-free loan for personal expenses with minimal documentation and fast disbursal.

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Driving License
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, or rent agreement
  • Income Proof (Salaried): Last 3 months salary slips, Form 16, and 3–6 months bank statements
  • Income Proof (Self-Employed): ITR for last 2 years and 6 months bank statements
  • Employment Proof: Appointment letter or employment ID for salaried applicants
  • Other: Passport-size photographs and signed loan application form

Business Loan

Fund working capital, purchase equipment, expand operations, or manage seasonal cash flow for your business.

  • KYC of Proprietor/Directors: Aadhaar, PAN, and address proof
  • Business Registration: GST certificate, Udyam/MSME registration, shop act license, or incorporation certificate
  • Financial Statements: ITR, balance sheet, and P&L for last 2–3 years
  • Bank Statements: Business current account statements for last 12 months
  • Business Proof: Partnership deed, MOA/AOA, or proprietorship declaration
  • Other: Business plan or project report, collateral documents (if secured loan), and board resolution

Car Loan

Finance a new or used car with tenure up to 7 years. The vehicle is hypothecated until the loan is repaid.

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Voter ID
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, or rental agreement
  • Income Proof: Salary slips (3 months) or ITR (2 years) with bank statements
  • Vehicle Documents: Proforma invoice or quotation for new car; RC and insurance for used car
  • Driving License: Valid driving license of the applicant
  • Other: Passport-size photographs, employment proof, and down payment receipt

Note: Document requirements may vary by lender, loan amount, and applicant profile. Self-attested copies are usually accepted at the time of application; originals may be required for verification.
