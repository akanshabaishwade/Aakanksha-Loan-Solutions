Loan Against Property Documents

Mortgage your residential or commercial property to access a high-value loan at lower interest rates.

  • Identity & Address Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or voter ID
  • Property Documents: Title deed, chain of ownership, approved plan, and encumbrance certificate (EC)
  • Property Tax & Utility Bills: Latest receipts as proof of ownership and occupancy
  • Income Proof: Salary slips or ITR for 2-3 years with 6-12 months bank statements
  • Business Proof (if applicable): GST registration, shop act license, or partnership deed
  • Other: Valuation report, photographs, and loan application form
Note: Requirements may vary by lender, loan amount, and applicant profile.
